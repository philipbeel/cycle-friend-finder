About Strava friend finder

What is it and why should I care?

Strava friend finder allows athletes to find active Strava users nearby. Moving somewhere new can be exciting, but also, at times, tedious. Use Strava friend finder to follow new people and explore new routes.

To use, simply enter your Strava ID and hit find.

Please note: You must enter your location on Strava to use this tool.

You can contribute to this project on github.

Created by @philipbeel.
